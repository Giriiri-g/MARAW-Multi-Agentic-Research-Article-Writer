About MARAW systems

MARAW - Multi-Agentic Research Article Writer is an AI-powered platform that automates the process of research paper writing through a coordinated team of specialized agents. Each agent is responsible for a specific task such as structuring content, drafting sections, managing citations, formatting, or reviewing the final output.

Built on the CrewAI framework and accelerated by the Groq Cloud Console, MARAW integrates open-source language models like LLaMA and Mistral to ensure accurate and high-quality generation. Developed by AI researchers and engineers, MARAW allows academics to focus on their ideas while the platform handles the technical work of writing.

Our Tools and Technologies

Crew AI

Multi-agent orchestration framework that coordinates specialized AI agents to work together seamlessly, enabling complex task automation and intelligent workflow management for research paper generation.

Groq Cloud Hosting

High-performance cloud infrastructure optimized for AI workloads, providing lightning-fast inference speeds and scalable computing resources for demanding language model applications.

Llama 3 Model Family

State-of-the-art large language model delivering exceptional performance in text generation, reasoning, and complex problem-solving across diverse academic and research domains.

LaTeX Generation

Automated academic document formatting system that generates publication-ready papers with proper citations, mathematical expressions, and professional layouts meeting journal standards.

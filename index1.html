About MARAW systems

MARAW - Multi-Agentic Research Article Writer is an AI-powered platform that automates the process of research paper writing through a coordinated team of specialized agents. Each agent is responsible for a specific task such as structuring content, drafting sections, managing citations, formatting, or reviewing the final output.

Built on the CrewAI framework and accelerated by the Groq Cloud Console, MARAW integrates open-source language models like LLaMA and Mistral to ensure accurate and high-quality generation. Developed by AI researchers and engineers, MARAW allows academics to focus on their ideas while the platform handles the technical work of writing.

About RWrite

RWrite is an agentic AI platform that automates research paper writing through a team of intelligent, task-specific agents working collaboratively. From structuring and drafting to citation, formatting, and review, each agent plays a specialized role. Designed to streamline academic writing, RWrite helps researchers focus on ideas — while the AI handles the rest.

RWrite is powered by the Groq Cloud Console for ultra-fast inference and built using the CrewAI framework to coordinate a multi-agent system. It integrates open-source language models like LLaMA and Mistral for specialized language generation tasks. Developed by a team of AI researchers and engineers, RWrite brings intelligent collaboration to every stage of the research pipeline.
